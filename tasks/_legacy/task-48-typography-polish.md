# Task 48 – Typography Polish

Goal

Refine typography hierarchy and text rhythm for better consistency across sections.

Scope

Section labels, lead text, supporting copy, footer labels, and CTA copy.

Files to Modify

index.html
styles.css

Changes

Add reusable typography helper classes where they reduce repetition.

Standardize section intros, lead paragraph widths, and small label presentation.

Keep all sizing aligned with design-system.md tokens.

Constraints

Do not replace the site font.

Do not introduce ad hoc font sizes that duplicate existing tokens without reason.

Avoid changing approved content solely for styling purposes.

Acceptance Criteria

Lead text, small labels, and secondary copy share consistent classes.

Typography remains readable across breakpoints.

The stylesheet relies on token-based typography patterns rather than one-off text rules.
