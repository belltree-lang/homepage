A clean editorial product catalog sheet for design asset extraction.
Pure solid white background, hex #FFFFFF, no gradient, no texture, no shadow.
Each item delicate hand-drawn illustration, centered, no tilt, no labels.
Style: refined editorial in the spirit of Kinfolk Magazine, Cereal, &Premium. NOT Japanese washi, NOT seasonal, NOT clinical, NOT cartoonish.
Drawing: thin terracotta outlines (#C27A65), soft watercolor wash on focus points. Closed-eye serene expressions, never distressed — frailty here is depicted as quiet fatigue, not despair.
Palette: terracotta #C27A65, soft wash #E3A48F, slate blue #1A365D, warm off-white #FCFCFA. NO bright colors.
Layout: 2 columns × 3 rows = 6 illustrations. Gutter ~25%, outer margin ~30%.

Subject: scenes depicting the subtle signs of frailty in elderly home life, gentle editorial style matching the previous Parkinson's L sheet.

Variations (6 total):
1) 歩く速度の低下 — Side view of an elderly person walking slowly down a corridor with a cane, soft motion lines behind. Closed eyes, serene.
2) 趣味から離れる — A small still life: a closed book and a folded pair of reading glasses on a side table beside an empty armchair. No human figure.
3) 体重減少 — A simple bathroom scale on a wooden floor, with a single pair of slippers beside it. Editorial still life, no human figure.
4) 握力の低下 — Close-up of an elderly hand trying to twist a bottle cap, the fingers slightly curled. Wash on the knuckles. No face.
5) 共に歩く伴走 — Two figures walking side by side, one slightly older with a cane, the other a caregiver lightly supporting at the elbow. Both closed eyes, serene.
6) 取り戻す活力 — A small still life: a steaming cup of warm tea, a fresh sprig of greenery, and morning light streaming in from a nearby window. Editorial still life, no human figure.

Output canvas: 3072×2048 pixels (landscape).

Constraints: No needles, no clinical equipment. No Japanese washi, no hanko, no seasonal motifs. No bright red. Quiet, calm expressions throughout.
