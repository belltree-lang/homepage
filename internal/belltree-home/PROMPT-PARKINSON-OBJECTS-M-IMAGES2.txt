プロンプト（このまま images2.0 にコピペ）
================================================================

A clean editorial product catalog sheet for design asset extraction.
Pure solid white background, hex #FFFFFF, absolutely no gradient, no texture, no shadow, no glow.
Each item is a delicate hand-drawn illustration, centered in its grid cell, no tilt.
Items evenly spaced, none overlapping, no labels, no numbers, no captions, no watermarks.

Style: refined editorial hand-drawn illustration in the spirit of Kinfolk Magazine, Cereal, &Premium. NOT Japanese washi, NOT seasonal motifs, NOT clinical, NOT cartoonish. Western lifestyle editorial sensibility.
Drawing technique: thin terracotta outlines (#C27A65), soft watercolor wash highlights in slightly deeper terracotta on small focal areas, otherwise clean white. Same line quality and color palette as the previous Parkinson's L sheet (elderly figures, pocket watch, house and hospital scene).

Muted warm color palette only:
- terracotta outline (#C27A65)
- soft terracotta wash (#E3A48F)
- slate blue secondary accent (#1A365D)
- warm off-white background (#FCFCFA)
NO bright colors, NO saturated reds, NO pastels.

Layout: 4 columns × 5 rows = 20 illustrations total.
Equal generous spacing between items (gutter ~20% of item size).
Outer margin ~25% of item size on all sides.
Items must be visually consistent in scale and line weight.
Each illustration is a single object or small still life, no large scenes.

Subject of this sheet: small editorial decoration objects related to home-visit care for Parkinson's disease patients — objects, fixtures, and small still lifes drawn from daily life, NOT clinical equipment.

Variations (one per cell, 20 total):

1) A wooden walking cane standing upright, slight watercolor wash on the curved handle.
2) A small medicine bottle with rounded shoulders, one capsule resting beside it.
3) A single oblong pill capsule lying on its side, viewed from above.
4) A small breakfast tray with a teacup and a folded napkin.
5) A simple wooden chair with a tall back, viewed from the front-side.
6) A bedside table with a small reading lamp on top and one folded book.
7) A window frame with two side curtains gently parted, soft light suggestion in the middle.
8) A small house silhouette with a pointed roof, one door, one window.
9) A small clinic building with a single cross over the entrance, two windows.
10) A curved path drawn with small dashed dots, leading from lower-left to upper-right.
11) An open notebook with a few faint handwritten lines and a fountain pen beside it.
12) A monthly calendar sheet showing a 5×7 grid with a single circled day.
13) A single leaf with visible veins, gently curved as if floating.
14) A soft cloud silhouette, plump and rounded.
15) A small sun with short rays, warm and modest.
16) An open hand, palm up, in a gesture of support.
17) A pair of indoor slippers placed neatly side by side, viewed from above.
18) A small heart, hand-drawn with a single slightly imperfect line.
19) A slightly opened door with warm light spilling out from inside.
20) A small potted plant with three leaves in a clay pot.

Output canvas: 3072×3072 pixels.
High resolution, crisp edges, sharp focus on every item, suitable for cut-out into transparent PNG.

Critical constraints:
- No needles, no syringes, no IV drips, no oxygen masks, no clinical paraphernalia.
- No Japanese washi, no hanko, no seasonal motifs (sakura, plum, seigaiha).
- No bright red, no saturated red — terracotta only.
- No human faces in this sheet (the hand in cell 16 is a hand only).
- All line work is consistent thin terracotta outline matching the previous L sheet.
