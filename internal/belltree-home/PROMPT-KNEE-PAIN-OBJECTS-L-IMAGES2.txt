A clean editorial product catalog sheet for design asset extraction.
Pure solid white background, hex #FFFFFF, no gradient, no texture, no shadow.
Each item delicate hand-drawn illustration, centered in its grid cell, no tilt, no labels, no numbers.
Style: refined editorial in the spirit of Kinfolk Magazine, Cereal, &Premium. NOT Japanese washi, NOT seasonal, NOT clinical, NOT cartoonish.
Drawing: thin terracotta outlines (#C27A65), soft watercolor wash on key emotion points (joints, painful spots) in deeper terracotta, otherwise clean white. Closed-eye serene expressions.
Palette: terracotta outline #C27A65, soft wash #E3A48F, slate blue accent #1A365D, warm off-white #FCFCFA. NO bright colors.
Layout: 2 columns × 3 rows = 6 illustrations. Gutter ~25%, outer margin ~30%. Consistent scale and line weight.

Subject: scenes of elderly people experiencing knee pain at home, in the same calm editorial style as the previous Parkinson's L sheet.

Variations (6 total):
1) 立ち上がりの痛み — An elderly person rising from a low chair, one hand pressing on the knee, body leaning forward. Wash highlight on the kneecap. Closed eyes, gentle.
2) 階段の下り — Side view of an elderly person descending a few wooden stairs, one hand on the rail, the down-foot tentative. Wash on the leading knee. Closed eyes.
3) 外出が億劫 — An elderly person sitting by a window, looking inward, slippers and a cardigan set aside. No outdoor scenery. Closed eyes, contemplative.
4) 膝の腫れ — Close-up of a knee with a faint watercolor wash and a small folded towel placed on a low table beside it. No human figure.
5) 共に歩く支え — Two figures walking arm-in-arm, side view, soft footsteps. Both closed eyes, serene.
6) 取り戻す歩み — A pair of well-worn walking shoes placed neatly side by side on a wooden floor, with a single fresh leaf nearby. Editorial still life.

Output canvas: 3072×2048 pixels (landscape). High resolution, crisp edges, suitable for transparent PNG cut-out.

Constraints: No needles, no clinical equipment. No Japanese washi, no hanko, no seasonal motifs. No bright red. All figures closed eyes, calm.
